# Systematic Literature Review (SLR) – Paper Collection Repository

This repository contains curated collections of reference papers compiled for multiple **Systematic Literature Review (SLR)** studies in **Applied Artificial Intelligence (AI)**. Each folder represents an independent SLR topic with its own research scope, selected literature, and bibliographic references.

The SLR studies archived in this repository were completed by students involved in research at the [Bioinformatics and Data Science Research Center (BDSRC)](https://bdsrc.binus.ac.id) and the [NVIDIA AI Research and Development Center (AIRDC)](https://airdc.binus.ac.id/), **Bina Nusantara (BINUS) University**, under the supervision of **Kuncahyo Setyo Nugroho** and **Bens Pardamean**.

*This repository is continuously updated as new SLR studies are completed and published.*

## 📁 Repository Structure

Each directory corresponds to a **specific SLR topic**. Inside each folder, you may find:

- `README.md` — A curated list of reviewed papers included in the SLR
- `references.bib` — BibTeX entries of the reviewed papers
